a sense of place and belonging


home does not fit the dictionary defintion that home is "a place one lives in permanently". what if you live in two different places or you constantly move around? what is your home then? we understand that one cannot force a feeling of belonging to create a sense of place; therefore, we are trying to create a community that encourages vunerablity in hopes to welcome belonging. below you will find different examples of what "home" can be, whether it's your friends, music, the forest, or even video games.
