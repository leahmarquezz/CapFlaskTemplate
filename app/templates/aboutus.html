about the desinger

hello and welcome my name is leah marquez. i am a senior at oakland technical high school. for our senior year we need to complete a research project. i chose to study a sense of place and belonging because being adopted has changed how i belong in the world. in the photo to the right you can see my biological mother holding me as a new born baby.


purpose

my goal is to create an online space where everyone can belong. i learned that a feeling of belonging cannot be forced but it can be welcomed. i am creating a space where people can share vulnerable parts of themselves in order to create real connections with others.


user interactivity

user story: nate is a student who during covid did nothing but lay in his bed. he was unable to go out to his favorite cafe or soccer field. his place of comfort was disrupted. he wants to learn how to recreate a place he feels comfortable and like he belongs. he opens up a sense of place website and is faces with three options. he can upload his own “home” or view others, he can do a challenge as an attempt to create different “homes”, or he can message with others discussing experiences. nate being quite competitive clicked the challenge option and was faced with a checklist of activities to complete.
